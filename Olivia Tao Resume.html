About

I'm currently in my second year of Industrial Design at Pratt Institute in Brooklyn, New York. I'm interested in how product design can seamlessly support the way we live and feel — and in the intersection of health, beauty, and wellness.

As a kid, I always gravitated toward hands-on activities — disassembling old jewelry, rebuilding LEGO sets, and building furniture. Those habits pushed me to explore how things are made and to collaborate across disciplines to advance a design.
